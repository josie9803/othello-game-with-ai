OTHELLO Game Rules:

1)Othello gameboard has around 64 discs which are black on one side and white on the other.
2)The objective of the game is to have majority of your colour discs on the board at the end of the game.
3)Game begins with the setup where black places two black discs and white places two white discs.
4)The game can be human vs human or human vs AI mode depending on the player preference.
5)A move consists of outflanking your opponent discs and then flipping the outflanked discs to your colour.
6)Outflanking means to place a disc on the board so that your opponent's row of discs is bordered by your colour.
7)A row may be made up of one or more discs.
8)Black disc always moves first.
9)When it is no longer possible for either player to move,the game is over. Discs are counted and the player with the majority of
the colour discs on the board is the winner.
10)It is possible for game to end before all 64 squares are filled.
